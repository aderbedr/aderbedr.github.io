About

I am a Software Engineer who strives to provide useful tools to better the world around me. I started programming in middle school, starting with simple C++ programs and designing websites for hobbies of mine. During the week, I work full-time as a developer for a investment analytic software company and on weekends, I try to explore the world around me through hiking, festivals or going with friends to new places. Portfolio

Stock Scanner

I wanted to determine if there was a correlation between keywords used in a headline (that contained a given company name) and the stock of that company in the next day. For instance, if the headline was "Foo Inc has best day ever", it would put a weight to "has", "best", "day" and "ever", then monitor the stock price change for Foo Inc over the next 24 hours. After the 24 hours has passed, it assigns a new weight to each word, averaging in the price change. After a few cycles of news headlines containing the word "best", it might predict with certainty that companies who are in headlines alongside the word "best" tend to go up in value over the upcoming day.
Language: C#
